Why CyberShield Wins

CyberShield creates decision evidence.

Most AI governance tools manage policies, dashboards, or model behavior. CyberShield focuses on whether a specific AI-generated recommendation is defensible enough to act on.

Positioning:
CyberShield is not AI governance theater. It turns AI advice into a reviewable, challengeable, exportable decision record.

What CyberShield does

  • Extracts claims from AI recommendations
  • Maps evidence to material claims
  • Identifies missing support
  • Classifies Risk If Wrong
  • Assigns confidence based on evidence state
  • Requires human review when risk demands it
  • Exports an AI Trust Decision Record

What CyberShield refuses to do

  • Confuse AI confidence with evidence
  • Treat SOC 2 as automatic approval
  • Treat policy as implementation proof
  • Hide unsupported claims
  • Replace accountable human review
  • Overclaim compliance, legal, or audit conclusions

Competitive distinction

Other tools often focus on

  • Policies
  • Dashboards
  • Generic risk registers
  • Model monitoring
  • Vendor questionnaires
  • Manual review checklists

CyberShield focuses on

  • One AI-generated recommendation
  • Its material claims
  • The evidence behind those claims
  • The consequences if wrong
  • The required reviewer
  • The defensible action record

Why buyers care

When AI advice becomes action, leadership will ask who reviewed it, based on what evidence, and why the organization acted. CyberShield produces that decision evidence before the action happens.
