CyberShield positioning:
CyberShield sits between enterprises and rapidly scaling autonomous systems. It helps leaders evaluate whether AI-assisted and automated actions should execute, who owns them, what evidence supports them, which policy boundary applies, and what consequence is created.

AI assurance overlay model:
CyberShield does not replace copilots, agents, AI assurance platforms, GRC, cloud AI governance, enterprise architecture, security operations, vendor risk, or compliance programs. It operationalizes the decision point where assurance, authority, policy, evidence, and consequence meet.
