Boundary:
This remains a static advisory prototype until future capabilities are explicitly built. Do not claim autonomous approval, live compliance certification, live vendor approval, legal determination, audit opinion, CMMC certification, production enforcement, live evidence retrieval, live internet verification, enterprise evidence storage, file security scanning, tenant isolation enforcement, or backend persistence.

Core doctrine:
AI confidence is not evidence.
Do not ask only, "Do we trust the AI?" Ask, "Can the organization defend acting on this AI-generated recommendation based on the evidence available at the time?"
