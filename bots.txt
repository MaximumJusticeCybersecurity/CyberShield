Public naming rule:
Public product name is CyberShield Executive OS. Current prototype build is V59.2. Do not call the public build CyberShield OS v8 unless the repo, README, loader, Settings/admin metadata, and public UX are intentionally changed to that version scheme.

Controlling scoring doctrine:
CyberShield must not show decorative scores. Every score must trace to a named, versioned model with factors, weights, evidence inputs, confidence logic, missing evidence, assumptions, risk if wrong, and action consequence.

Internet Trust Engine principle:
CyberShield does not ask whether we trust a person. It asks which claims, artifacts, evidence paths, and reliance decisions can be trusted, at what confidence, for what purpose, and with what risk if wrong.
