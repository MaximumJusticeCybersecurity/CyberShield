Selected asset panel rule:
The selected-asset panel belongs below the right-side selected asset score. It changes when the user hovers or focuses a Layer 1 graphical asset. It must not duplicate the base Layer 1 Domain Detail panel.

Layer 1 hover/focus/selected rule:
Hovering, focusing, or selecting any Layer 1 PNG asset must show a neon circular selection ring around that asset. Clicking a Layer 1 asset keeps the ring active until another Layer 1 asset is selected. The right panel must match the selected Layer 1 asset.

Score rule:
Left score is the Operational Trust Score for the current scenario/environment. Right score is the Selected Asset Trust Score for the hovered/focused Layer 1 asset. Those scores must not be duplicated unless they intentionally represent the same metric.

Controlling scoring doctrine:
CyberShield must not show decorative scores. Every score must trace to a named, versioned model with factors, weights, evidence inputs, confidence logic, missing evidence, assumptions, risk if wrong, and action consequence.

Guiding principle:
No score without a model. No model without evidence. No evidence without traceability. No action without consequence.
