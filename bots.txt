Public naming rule:
Public product name is CyberShield Executive OS. Current prototype build is V58. Do not call the public build CyberShield OS v8 unless the repo, README, loader, Settings/admin metadata, and public UX are intentionally changed to that version scheme.

Controlling scoring doctrine:
CyberShield must not show decorative scores. Every score must trace to a named, versioned model with factors, weights, evidence inputs, confidence logic, missing evidence, assumptions, risk if wrong, and action consequence.

Guiding principle:
No score without a model. No model without evidence. No evidence without traceability. No action without consequence.

Preferred product term: Trust Scoring Models.
Preferred architectural term: Trust Model Registry.
