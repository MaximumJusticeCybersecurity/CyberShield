Human accountability rule:
CyberShield does not make the human disappear. AI may generate outputs, recommendations, or action requests, but a named human authority remains accountable for final decision ownership, escalation, risk acceptance, or override.

Evidence rule:
CyberShield should preserve a replayable evidence packet for relevant decisions. Useful evidence includes action attempted, actor, target system, policy boundary, authority score, evidence score, consequence score, human owner, rationale, next action, timestamp, and prototype boundary.

Public prototype boundary:
The current GitHub Pages build is a lightweight static prototype. It is not yet connected to live enterprise telemetry, real identity systems, SIEM data, EDR, cloud control planes, GRC tooling, or production agent enforcement. Do not represent it as a fully deployed live enforcement engine.
