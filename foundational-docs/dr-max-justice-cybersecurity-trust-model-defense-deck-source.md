## Why this matters to CyberShield

The defense deck compresses the dissertation into a presentation form and highlights the strategic concepts behind CyberShield:

- zero trust creates organizational and human trust challenges
- some level of trust is required for communication and interoperability
- a trust model can be based on trustworthiness dimensions
- trust is connected to people, processes, technology, institutions, and leadership
- the research used executive-level cybersecurity perspectives
- the model is connected to credibility, applicability, consistency, and authenticity
