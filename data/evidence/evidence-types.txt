CyberShield Evidence Type Registry Source
Version: 0.1
Status: planning scaffold
Baseline: V51.1 Executive Story and CTA Cleanup

Purpose:
Define reusable evidence types used by scoring models, scenarios, reports, and onboarding.

Core Evidence Types:
- AI Use Policy
- Vendor Security Review
- Data Classification Policy
- Access Control Policy
- Owner Approval Record
- Decision Record
- Proof Pack Export
- Incident Response Plan
- Backup Validation Record
- Policy Review Record

Authenticity Evidence Types:
- official website verification
- authorized reseller list
- product ID or SKU validation
- warranty validation
- platform ad record
- marketplace abuse report
- executive likeness authorization
- payment destination verification
- customer complaint record
- legal/IP response record

Evidence Metadata:
- evidence_id
- evidence_type
- owner
- confidence
- freshness
- source
- related_model
- related_scenario
- status
- limitations

Builder Rule:
Every score and decision should identify supporting evidence and missing evidence.
