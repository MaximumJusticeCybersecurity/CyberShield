CyberShield Report Registry Source
Version: 0.1
Status: planning scaffold
Baseline: V51.1 Executive Story and CTA Cleanup

Purpose:
Define exportable reports and proof artifacts.

Initial Reports:

1. Executive Briefing Summary
Audience: executives, board, advisors
Purpose: concise trust-transfer artifact
Required content: risky action, CyberShield decision, why it matters, owner, next action, primary scores, limitations

2. TrustMap / Authenticity Trust Summary
Audience: executives, operations, legal/IP, advisors
Purpose: show trust surface, impersonation risk, authenticity gaps, owner paths
Required content: TrustMap state, authenticity score, Trust Under Attack, Official Source Verification Gate, evidence gaps

3. Operational Trust Roadmap
Audience: executive owner, CISO/vCISO, CIO/CTO, CFO
Purpose: show score-improvement roadmap and owner accountability
Required content: priority actions, owners, score lift, evidence needs, timeline, advisory path

4. Proof Pack
Audience: governance, audit, board, advisor, pilot team
Purpose: defensible decision record and evidence summary
Required content: decision, model versions, evidence assumptions, missing evidence, limitations, boundary language

5. Evidence Register
Audience: CISO/vCISO, CIO/CTO, governance owner
Purpose: show evidence state, owners, gaps, freshness, confidence
Required content: evidence types, confidence, freshness, owner, model dependency

Required Boundary:
All shareable reports must include capability boundary and model status labels.
