CyberShield Decision Assurance

Defensible records for AI-assisted decisions.

CyberShield helps organizations document the evidence, gaps, risk, rationale, and human accountability behind decisions influenced by AI before those decisions are challenged.

Trust Decision RecordPackage HubRun Workflow
Core question:
What decision are you making today using AI that you cannot currently defend?

Why it matters

Teams are using AI to summarize vendors, interpret controls, recommend security changes, draft policies, and brief executives. CyberShield focuses on the record that explains why a decision was trusted, challenged, revised, or escalated.

What CyberShield creates

  • Trust Decision Records
  • Executive Decision Assurance Briefs
  • Evidence-gap summaries
  • Risk If Wrong summaries
  • Human review triggers

First wedges

  • Vendor risk for broad executive demos
  • Security risk acceptance for CISO and vCISO buyers
  • Compliance mapping for GRC and regulated buyers

What it is not

CyberShield is not a black-box trust score, dashboard-first governance tool, or autonomous decision-maker. It structures evidence and accountability so humans can make better-defended decisions.
