Trust Decision Record

The record that explains why a decision was trusted.

A Trust Decision Record documents the claim, evidence, gaps, Risk If Wrong, confidence rationale, review path, and accountable human decision behind an AI-assisted recommendation.

Decision AssuranceCreate RecordExecutive Brief
Artifact rule:
The record is the product. The dashboard is only the workbench.

What the record captures

  • Original AI-assisted recommendation
  • Material claims
  • Required evidence
  • Evidence provided
  • Missing or weak support
  • Contradictions and caveats

What the record decides

  • Risk If Wrong
  • Confidence based on evidence state
  • Human review requirement
  • Recommended next action
  • Decision owner
  • Record defensibility posture

First subtype

The first CyberShield workflow produces an AI Trust Decision Record, a Trust Decision Record focused on AI-generated security, compliance, and vendor-risk recommendations.

Why buyers care

When a decision is reviewed later, the Trust Decision Record shows what evidence existed and what review path was used.
