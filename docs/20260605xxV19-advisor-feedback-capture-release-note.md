## Intended Use

Use V19 after an advisor or friendly-buyer walkthrough to capture:

- Whether the problem made sense
- Whether the vendor-risk demo made the risk obvious
- Whether the Trust Decision Record felt valuable
- Whether the reviewer saw a paid pilot path
- What was confusing
- What would make the reviewer dismiss it
- What would make the product stronger

## Buyer Signal Rubric

The rubric scores:

- AI-assisted decisions already happening
- Risk owner clarity
- Evidence pain
- Budget owner path
- Pilot candidate
- Urgency

## Product Rule

Do not confuse enthusiasm with buying signal.

A strong signal requires a named use case, owner, evidence pain, and plausible pilot path.
