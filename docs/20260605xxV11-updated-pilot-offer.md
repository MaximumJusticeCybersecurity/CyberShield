# 20260605xxV11 Updated Pilot Offer

## Offer Name

```text
CyberShield AI-Assisted Decision Assurance Sprint
```

## Core Question

```text
What decision are you making today using AI that you cannot currently defend?
```

## Purpose

Create Trust Decision Records for a small set of AI-assisted recommendations or decisions.

## Scope

```text
3 to 5 AI-assisted recommendations or decisions
2 to 4 weeks
Vendor risk, security risk acceptance, or compliance mapping focus
```

## Lead Deliverable

```text
Executive Decision Assurance Brief
```

## Supporting Deliverables

- Trust Decision Records
- Evidence-gap summary
- Risk If Wrong summary
- Human review trigger summary
- Recommended decision controls

## Best First Wedges

- Vendor risk for broad demos
- Security risk acceptance for CISO and vCISO buyers
- Compliance mapping for GRC and regulated buyers

## Buyer-Facing Position

```text
CyberShield creates defensible records for AI-assisted decisions.
```

## Boundary

The sprint structures evidence, gaps, risk, rationale, and human review. It does not replace accountable decision-makers.

## Close

```text
Can we identify 3 to 5 AI-assisted decisions your organization may rely on soon and produce Trust Decision Records showing the evidence, gaps, risk, and review path before those decisions are challenged?
```
