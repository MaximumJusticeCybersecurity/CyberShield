# 20260605xxV17 Pilot Close Sheet

## Pilot Name

CyberShield AI-Assisted Decision Assurance Sprint

## Best First Ask

What 3 to 5 AI-assisted recommendations is your organization likely to rely on soon?

## Pilot Scope

- 3 to 5 AI-assisted recommendations
- Vendor-risk, security risk acceptance, or compliance mapping focus
- Trust Decision Records for each recommendation
- Executive Decision Assurance Brief
- Evidence-gap summary
- Human review trigger summary

## Best First Wedge

Vendor risk remains the strongest first demo because it is easy to understand and already has evidence-based review patterns.

## Buyer-Specific Pilot Framing

### Vendor Risk

Review 3 to 5 AI-assisted vendor approval recommendations and produce Trust Decision Records showing claims, evidence, gaps, contradictions, Risk If Wrong, confidence, and review path.

### CISO / vCISO

Review 3 to 5 AI-assisted security recommendations, risk acceptances, or control-change recommendations before action.

### GRC / Compliance

Review 3 to 5 AI-assisted compliance mappings or control conclusions and separate framework relevance from compliance proof.

## Deliverables

- Trust Decision Record for each recommendation
- Executive Decision Assurance Brief
- Evidence-gap summary
- Risk If Wrong summary
- Human review requirement summary
- Recommended decision controls

## Buyer Outcome

The buyer can see which AI-assisted recommendations are defensible, which are not defensible as written, what evidence is missing, who should review, and what action is recommended.

## Close Language

The first pilot does not require integrations or broad deployment. Give CyberShield 3 to 5 AI-assisted recommendations, and we will produce the records your team would want if those decisions were questioned later.

## Do Not Overclaim

Do not claim the static prototype is production-ready.

Do not claim CyberShield approves vendors.

Do not claim CyberShield certifies compliance.

Do not claim CyberShield replaces legal, procurement, compliance, security, or executive accountability.

CyberShield creates defensible decision evidence and a reviewable record.
