# CyberShield AI Decision Assurance

## One-Page Sales Leave-Behind

### The Problem

Organizations are already acting on AI-generated recommendations in security, compliance, vendor risk, procurement, and governance.

The danger is not that AI gives advice.

The danger is that confident AI advice becomes enterprise action before anyone proves the recommendation is defensible.

### The Core Belief

```text
AI confidence is not evidence.
```

A recommendation is not defensible until its claims are understood, material evidence is mapped, gaps are disclosed, Risk If Wrong is classified, and the right human reviewer accepts, rejects, or constrains the decision.

### What CyberShield Does

CyberShield turns AI-generated recommendations into AI Trust Decision Records.

Before your team acts on AI advice, CyberShield shows:

- What the AI recommended
- What claims the AI made
- Which claims are material
- What evidence supports each claim
- What evidence is missing, weak, stale, incomplete, or contradicted
- What happens if the AI is wrong
- Who must review it
- What action is defensible
