# 20260605xxV9 ATDR Pilot Proposal Worksheet

## Offer

CyberShield AI Recommendation Assurance Sprint

## Prepared By

Maximum Justice Cybersecurity

## Objective

Turn selected AI-generated security, compliance, or vendor-risk recommendations into AI Trust Decision Records.

## Recommended Scope

- 3 to 5 AI-generated recommendations
- 2 to 4 weeks
- One executive readout
- Security, compliance, or vendor-risk focus

## Client Inputs

For each recommendation, collect:

- Recommendation text
- Intended use
- Decision context
- Available evidence
- Decision owner
- Known deadline

## Workflow

1. Intake recommendation and context
2. Extract claims
3. Map evidence
4. Identify missing support
5. Classify Risk If Wrong
6. Assign confidence band
7. Identify human review requirement
8. Produce AI Trust Decision Record
9. Summarize findings

## Deliverables

- AI Trust Decision Records
- Evidence-gap summary
- Risk If Wrong summary
- Human review trigger summary
- Executive findings brief
- Recommended workflow controls

## Candidate Use Cases

- AI vendor approval
- Vendor-risk recommendation
- Vulnerability risk acceptance
- Compliance control mapping
- Security control recommendation
- Policy adequacy review
- Customer-facing AI assurance language

## Success Criteria

The client can identify what the AI recommended, what evidence supports it, what is missing, what could happen if wrong, who must review it, and what action is defensible.

## Follow-On Options

- Additional recommendation batches
- Ongoing AI Trust Decision Record support
- AI vendor review workflow
- Compliance recommendation review workflow
- Security risk acceptance review workflow
- Production CyberShield platform pilot
