# 20260605xxV11 Advisor Feedback Synthesis

## Purpose

Summarize the post-merge advisor feedback and convert it into product direction.

## Core Finding

The strongest market language is not generic AI trust.

The sharper category is:

```text
Defensible AI-assisted decisions
```

## Updated Question

Do not lead with:

```text
Would you buy an AI Trust Analyzer?
```

Lead with:

```text
What decision are you making today using AI that you cannot currently defend?
```

## Updated Product Position

```text
CyberShield creates Trust Decision Records for AI-assisted decisions.
```

## Updated Category

```text
Decision Assurance
```

## Updated Artifact

```text
Trust Decision Record
```

Use AI Trust Decision Record as a workflow-specific subtype.

## Wedge Strategy

Default broad demo:

```text
Vendor Risk
```

CISO and vCISO demo:

```text
Security Risk Acceptance
```

GRC and regulated buyer demo:

```text
Compliance Mapping
```

## Pilot Direction

Keep the pilot small:

```text
3 to 5 recommendations
2 to 4 weeks
Trust Decision Records
Executive Decision Assurance Brief
```

## Main Risk To Avoid

Avoid anything that feels like:

```text
AI judging AI
black-box trust scoring
dashboard theater
autonomous decision-making
```

## What To Lead With

Lead with:

```text
evidence
rationale
risk
review
accountability
exportable decision record
```

## Strategic Consensus

The record is the product.

The executive brief is the buying artifact.

The first commercial motion should be a fixed-scope sprint.
