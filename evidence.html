Evidence is not equal.

CyberShield must help reviewers distinguish independent proof, contractual language, test evidence, self-attestation, stale artifacts, screenshots without context, and unsupported AI conclusions.

Uploaded files are browser-local in this prototype. This page documents the evidence model. It does not claim enterprise evidence storage, scanning, retention, or server-side processing.

Strong evidence

Independent reportControl test resultContract clauseSystem-generated log

Useful when scope, date, authority, and traceability match the claim.

Weak evidence

Vendor assertionPolicy onlyScreenshotMarketing whitepaper

May support context, but rarely proves implementation or approval by itself.

Blocking issues

Scope mismatchContradictionStale reportMissing owner

These prevent a record from being decision-ready unless disclosed, remediated, or reviewed.
