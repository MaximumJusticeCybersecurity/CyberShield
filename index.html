How it works

1. Load an AI recommendation

Start with the recommendation your team is considering.

2. Claims are separated

CyberShield breaks polished AI output into reviewable claims.

3. Evidence gaps are exposed

Unsupported assumptions, weak evidence, and contradictions become visible.

4. Risk If Wrong is classified

The record shows what could go wrong if the recommendation is trusted too quickly.

5. Human review is triggered

Escalation is called out where accountable review is needed.

6. A decision record is created

The output is a defensible AI Trust Decision Record.
