2026060419V1 Human Review Workflow Expansion

Human review is the control layer.

CyberShield must preserve the difference between what the system recommends and what an accountable person decides. That separation is the heart of defensibility.

Open ATDR WorkbenchOpen Executive Brief
Decision rule:
High-risk recommendations, unresolved evidence gaps, material conflicts, or compliance-impacting decisions require human review before acceptance.

Reviewer roles

  • vCISO
  • Security SME
  • Compliance owner
  • Vendor-risk owner
  • Legal counsel
  • Data protection officer
  • Procurement owner
  • Executive sponsor
  • System owner

Allowed decisions

  • Approve
  • Approve with caveat
  • Request evidence
  • Revise recommendation
  • Escalate
  • Reject
  • Quarantine or hold for review

Notes required when

  • Approving with caveat
  • Rejecting
  • Overriding CyberShield
  • Approving High Risk If Wrong
  • Accepting residual risk
  • Closing a missing evidence gap

Review state model
