AI-Assisted Decision Assurance Sprint

A fixed-scope sprint that turns 3 to 5 AI-assisted recommendations into Trust Decision Records and an Executive Decision Assurance Brief.

Decision AssuranceTrust Decision RecordBuyer Paths
First ask:
What decision are you making today using AI that you cannot currently defend?

Scope

  • 3 to 5 AI-assisted recommendations or decisions
  • 2 to 4 weeks
  • Vendor risk, security risk acceptance, or compliance mapping focus
  • Executive Decision Assurance Brief

Deliverables

  • Trust Decision Records
  • Evidence-gap summary
  • Risk If Wrong summary
  • Human review trigger summary
  • Recommended decision controls

Boundary

The sprint structures evidence, gaps, risk, rationale, and human review. It does not replace accountable decision-makers.
