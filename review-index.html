Review thesis

The AI recommendation sounds reasonable because it cites SOC 2, encryption, and low risk. CyberShield shows why approving the vendor without more evidence and human review would be risky and hard to defend.

Do not claim

  • Production readiness.
  • Live LLM-backed analysis.
  • Production CRM infrastructure.
  • Compliance certification.
  • Automatic vendor approval from SOC 2.
  • Framework mapping as compliance proof.
